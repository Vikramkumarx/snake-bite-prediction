‚ö° Immediate Actions
‚è∞ TIME IS CRITICAL!

Snake bites can be life-threatening. Every second counts. Call 911 first, then follow these steps.

  1. Call 911 Immediately
    Don't wait to identify the snake. Get professional help right away.
  2. Keep Calm and Still
    Movement increases venom circulation. Keep the victim calm and limit movement.
  3. Remove Jewelry & Tight Clothing
    Swelling may occur. Remove rings, watches, and tight clothing near the bite.
  4. Position Below Heart
    Keep the bitten area below heart level to slow venom spread.
  5. Clean the Wound
    Gently clean with soap and water if possible. Cover with clean bandage.
  6. Mark the Swelling
    Use a pen to mark the edge of swelling every 15 minutes to track progression.
  7. Take a Photo
    If safe, photograph the snake from a distance for identification.
